About

I am Juan Francisco Lebrero, an AI enthusiast dedicated to exploring how artificial intelligence can transform our reality. Currently, I am an NLP Scientist at Mercado Libre within the LLMs team, where I focus on training, fine-tuning, and distilling Large Language Models to drive direct impact across the ecosystem. Simultaneously, I am pursuing a B.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence Engineering at the University of San Andrés (UdeSA), where I delve into the frontiers of this discipline.

A Journey through Innovation

In my current role at Mercado Libre, I work on:

  • Training, fine-tuning, and distilling LLMs with direct impact on products and users.
  • Designing data architecture, metrics, and evaluations to ensure robust, scalable, and high-quality models.
  • Building and evolving the large-scale AI model training and serving platform.

Additionally, as a Research Assistant at LiNAR, I focus on one of the most compelling applications of computer vision: the non-invasive assessment of embryos in IVF processes, developing models to predict viability using advanced time-lapse imaging techniques.

I also contributed to the data science efforts at SOFLEX, where we faced the challenge of processing and understanding human language within the context of public safety. I directed the training of language models tailored to Argentine Spanish and the development of intelligence platforms that analyze millions of incidents in real-time.

My experience also spans the industrial and educational sectors. At Papelera San Andrés de Giles, I implemented multimodal vision systems for product detection. At UK University Mexico, I led the AI strategy to optimize educational content production. Previously, at Notimation, I explored agent orchestration and RAG systems to streamline human-information interaction.

Perspective and Tools

I view programming and model design as essential tools for solving complex problems. I move fluently between languages such as Python, C/C++, and CUDA, building solutions on modern cloud infrastructures and databases. My academic background has allowed me to specialize in NLP, Computer Vision, and generative models, always with a practical focus.

Beyond code, I enjoy the challenges of competitive programming and constant innovation. I have had the honor of qualifying for the ICPC regionals and being recognized in various technical competitions—milestones that reflect my commitment to excellence and continuous learning.
